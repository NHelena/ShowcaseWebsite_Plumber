Cost estimates

Hours estimates

Estimates can be carried out Monday-Friday between 8 AM and 5 PM.
An appointment can be made, at a time that is the most convenient for you. Estimates outside of these hours can also be arranged. Written estimates will be posted - or emailed - within 48 hours of an estimator's visit and are valid for 6 months.

For urgent work, estimates can be arranged promptly and work can then commence immediately.

Appointments estimates

I will evaluate the work there's to do, & provide you with a detailed description of the estimate, a full breakdown of the costs and organise a schedule.

I will also keep you updated on all the carried out work & inform you of its progress.
